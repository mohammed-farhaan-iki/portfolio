FLeet Manager

I created a user-friendly, mobile-optimized web app for a fishing and sales business, designed to work smoothly on phones with a clean, simple look (white background, card-style layout, and large, easy-to-tap buttons). It helps manage the business on the go with features like:
Boat Trip Tracking: Log boat trips by entering the boat’s name, code, trip date, departure time, location, and fish caught.

Fish Catch Logging: Record fish details (type, size, weight) for each trip and keep track of unsold fish inventory.

Sales Management: Track sales to customers, including fish type, weight, price per kg, and total amount. It also stores customer names, payment methods (cash, credit, GPay), paid amounts, and tracks unpaid balances. Generate PDF invoices for sales.

Expense Tracking: Add expenses brke fuel or salaries with details (name, date, amount, category) and upload receipts if needed. Supports one-time or recurring expenses.

Reports & Dashboard: View daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly summaries of fish caught, sales, expenses, top-selbrng fish, and customer balances. Filter by boat, customer, or fish type, and export reports as PDFs.

Customers: See detailed sales history and running balances for each customer.
The app is built to be intuitive for use in the field, with easy navigation via tabs for Boats & Trips, Fish Inventory, Sales, Expenses, Reports, and Customers. It’s designed for a single user now but can grow to support multiple users later. No login or notifications are included to keep it simple.
